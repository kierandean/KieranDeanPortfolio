THE PAYDAY LOAN TRAP

Studies from across the UK expose the financial difficulties among a lot of students and highlights how it impacts diet, mental health and replationships. It is easy to see why students regularly turn to solutions such as credit cards, pay-day loans, drug trials and 'adult work' throughout the year.

Payday loans are notorious for their exteremly high interest rates, so much so that many students end up in more debt. That's a vast amount of money from students who are already in debt.

'Kiwi Lending' is a two-pronged propsal for an alternative platform where students can borrow money with much lower interests. Where it differs from pay-day loans is that it will operate as a peer-to-peer service meaning not only can students borrow money, they can also loan money to other students through the app.
